## What is Razem?

Razem (meaning "together" in Polish) is a unified financial platform designed to bring all your financial services into one seamless experience. Built by **Digital Edge Solutions (DES)**, Razem provides:

- **Payment Processing** — Fast, secure transactions for individuals and businesses
- **Account Management** — Full control of your financial accounts through the My Razem portal
- **Developer API** — RESTful API for integrating Razem's financial services into your applications
- **Real-time Notifications** — Instant updates on account activity and transactions
