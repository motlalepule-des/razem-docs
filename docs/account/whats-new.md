# What's New

Recent updates and improvements to the Razem platform.

{: .note }
Developer? See the [API Changelog](/razem-docs/docs/reference/changelog) for a full technical history of API changes.

---

## April 2025 — Platform Launch

Razem is now live for South African businesses and individuals.

### Payments

- Send EFT payments to any South African bank account
- Real-Time Payments (RTP) for instant transfers
- Schedule payments for future dates
- Bulk payment uploads via the portal

### Account Management

- Individual and business account registration
- KYC verification with tiered account limits
- Downloadable PDF and CSV statements
- Beneficiary management with saved recipients

### Security

- Two-factor authentication (SMS, authenticator app, or biometrics)
- Device management — view and remove trusted devices
- Login notifications for unrecognised sign-ins
- Account freeze for immediate protection

---

## Coming Soon

The following features are in development:

- **Recurring payments** — Set up automated scheduled payments
- **Payment links** — Share a link to request money
- **QR code payments** — Pay or receive by scanning a QR code
- **Multi-currency** — USD and EUR support
