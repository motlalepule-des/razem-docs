## Registration

### Step 1: Create Your Account

Visit [account.razem.co.za](https://account.razem.co.za) and click **Create Account**.

Provide:
- Full name (as per ID document)
- Email address
- Mobile number
- Password (minimum 12 characters, must include uppercase, number, and symbol)

### Step 2: Verify Your Email

Check your inbox for a verification email from `noreply@razem.co.za`. Click the verification link within 24 hours.

### Step 3: Verify Your Mobile Number

Enter the 6-digit OTP sent to your mobile number. This links your phone for two-factor authentication.

## KYC Verification

Razem is a registered financial services provider and is required by law to verify customer identities (FICA compliance).

### Required Documents

**For Individuals:**
- South African ID (green barcoded ID or Smart ID card) OR passport
- Proof of address (not older than 3 months) — utility bill, bank statement, or lease agreement

**For Businesses:**
- Company registration documents (CK1/CIPC certificate)
- Proof of business address
- Certified ID copies of all directors
- Company bank statement (last 3 months)

### Verification Process

1. Log in to your account
2. Navigate to **Settings → Identity Verification**
3. Upload the required documents
4. Allow 1-3 business days for review
5. You'll receive an email notification once verified

{: .note }
While KYC is pending, you can receive funds but payments are limited to R5,000 per day.

## Account Limits

| Limit | Unverified | KYC Verified | Business |
|:---|:---|:---|:---|
| Daily payments | R 5,000 | R 50,000 | R 500,000 |
| Monthly payments | R 15,000 | R 500,000 | Unlimited |
| Minimum balance | None | None | None |

To increase limits beyond the standard tier, contact [support@razem.co.za](mailto:support@razem.co.za).

## Profile Settings

After setup, configure your profile:

1. **Notification Preferences** — Choose email, SMS, or push notification for each event type
2. **Default Payment Reference** — Set a default reference for outgoing payments
3. **Statement Preferences** — Choose PDF or CSV format for statements
4. **Language** — Select your preferred language (English, Afrikaans, Zulu)
5. **Time Zone** — Set your local time zone for transaction timestamps
