## Sending Money

### One-Time Payment

1. Click **Pay** from the dashboard or navigation menu
2. Select **New Payment** or choose a saved beneficiary
3. Enter payment details:
   - Amount (ZAR)
   - Payment date (today or a future date)
   - Reference (for your records)
   - Recipient reference (shown on their statement)
4. Review the payment summary including any applicable fees
5. Confirm with your OTP or biometric authentication
6. Save as a recurring payment if needed

### Scheduled Payments

Set up payments in advance:

1. Follow steps 1-4 above
2. Select **Schedule Payment** instead of confirming immediately
3. Choose the payment date
4. Optionally enable recurring (daily, weekly, monthly, or custom)

### Bulk Payments

For business accounts, upload multiple payments at once:

1. Navigate to **Payments → Bulk Upload**
2. Download the CSV template
3. Fill in payment details (one row per payment)
4. Upload the completed CSV
5. Review and confirm

The CSV format:

```
name,account_number,bank_code,account_type,amount,reference,recipient_reference
John Doe,1234567890,632005,current,500.00,Invoice 001,Payment from Acme
```

## Receiving Money

Share your banking details to receive payments:

- **Account Name:** Your registered name
- **Account Number:** Shown in **Settings → Banking Details**
- **Bank:** Razem Financial Services
- **Branch Code:** Available in your account settings
- **Account Type:** Current

## Transaction History

View your complete transaction history:

1. Navigate to **Transactions** from the main menu
2. Use filters to narrow results:
   - Date range
   - Transaction type (debit/credit)
   - Amount range
   - Status

### Exporting Transactions

Export transactions for accounting or record-keeping:

1. Apply desired filters
2. Click **Export**
3. Choose format: **CSV** (for spreadsheets) or **PDF** (for printing)
